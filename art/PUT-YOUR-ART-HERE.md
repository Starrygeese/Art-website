# Drop your art in this folder

This folder ships with the website, so only put **finished pieces you want the public to
see** (watermarked versions are fine).

## Naming — keep it simple
- **banner.jpg** (or .png) — the wide piece for the top of the site. Pick something with
  a strong face/expression; wide crops work best.
- Everything else: number them in the order you want them shown, plus a short name:
  `01-dragon-knight.jpg`, `02-elf-portrait.jpg`, `03-oc-sheet.jpg` …
  **01 = your best piece** (it gets the biggest spot).

Don't stress the names — Claude looks at every image with its own eyes and designs
around what it sees. The numbers are just your way of saying what deserves the spotlight.

## Format
- JPG or PNG. Big is fine (Claude will make web-sized copies + thumbnails).
- 6–10 pieces is the sweet spot for launch. You can add more anytime.

## When you're done
Tell Claude: **"my art is in the folder"** — it will review each piece, build the
gallery (with click-to-enlarge), place the banner, and restyle the site around your
work's colors.
